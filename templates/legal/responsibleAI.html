1. Purpose

Genelis utilizes artificial intelligence ("AI") technologies to enhance learning experiences and provide educational assistance to users.

This AI Content Disclaimer & Responsible AI Policy explains:

  • How AI is used within Genelis.
  • The limitations of AI-generated content.
  • User responsibilities when relying on AI-generated outputs.
  • Genelis's commitment to responsible AI usage.

This Policy should be read together with the Genelis Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

2. How AI Is Used in Genelis

Genelis may use artificial intelligence technologies, including third-party AI models and services, to generate or assist in generating:

  • Chapter notes
  • Subject notes
  • Study summaries
  • Mock tests
  • Practice questions
  • Answer explanations
  • Doubt-solving responses
  • Revision materials
  • Educational recommendations
  • Learning insights
  • Additional educational content introduced in the future

AI systems are intended to support and enhance learning, not replace independent study, teachers, textbooks, or educational institutions.

3. AI-Generated Content May Contain Errors

While Genelis takes reasonable steps to improve content quality, users acknowledge that AI-generated content may occasionally contain:

  • Inaccuracies
  • Omissions
  • Typographical errors
  • Outdated information
  • Incomplete explanations
  • Incorrect assumptions
  • Ambiguous responses
  • Misinterpretation of user queries

No AI system can guarantee perfect accuracy at all times.

Users should independently verify important information before relying upon it.

4. No Guarantee of Accuracy

Genelis does not guarantee that AI-generated content will always be:

  • Accurate
  • Complete
  • Current
  • Error-free
  • Suitable for a specific examination
  • Suitable for a specific educational purpose

Educational content should be cross-verified using:

  • NCERT textbooks
  • Official CBSE resources
  • Teachers
  • School materials
  • Other trusted educational sources

5. No Guarantee of Academic Results

Genelis does not guarantee:

  • Examination success
  • Board examination scores
  • Academic rankings
  • Admissions
  • Scholarships
  • Competitive examination outcomes
  • Learning outcomes

Academic performance depends on numerous factors including:

  • Student effort
  • Study habits
  • Educational environment
  • Teacher guidance
  • Examination conditions
  • Prior knowledge

Use of AI tools alone cannot guarantee academic success.

6. AI Is an Educational Assistant, Not a Substitute for Teachers

Genelis is designed to supplement learning.

AI-generated responses should not be considered a replacement for:

  • Teachers
  • Tutors
  • Schools
  • Coaching institutions
  • Educational authorities

Students should continue to engage with formal educational resources and professional guidance.

7. Responsible Use of AI

Users agree to use Genelis's AI-powered features responsibly.

Users shall not:

  • Attempt to misuse AI systems.
  • Use AI outputs for unlawful purposes.
  • Submit malicious prompts.
  • Attempt to exploit system vulnerabilities.
  • Generate misleading or harmful content.
  • Use automated tools to abuse AI features.

Genelis reserves the right to restrict access to AI features where misuse is detected.

8. Student Responsibilities

Users are encouraged to:

  • Verify important academic information.
  • Review generated answers critically.
  • Use multiple learning resources.
  • Apply independent judgment.
  • Report suspected errors where possible.

Students remain responsible for how they use AI-generated content.

Genelis cannot guarantee that every generated response will meet a student's specific expectations.

9. Examination and Academic Integrity

Genelis encourages ethical learning practices.

Users should not use AI-generated content to:

  • Engage in academic dishonesty.
  • Violate school policies.
  • Misrepresent AI-generated work as entirely original where disclosure is required.
  • Cheat during examinations or assessments.

Users remain responsible for complying with the rules and academic integrity standards applicable to their educational institutions.

10. Human Review of Content

Genelis may periodically review certain educational materials, templates, prompts, or outputs for:

  • Quality assurance
  • Error correction
  • Service improvement
  • Safety purposes

However, not every AI-generated response is individually reviewed by a human before being presented to users.

Users should therefore independently assess the suitability of generated content.

Genelis may utilize AI services provided by third-party technology providers.

Such providers may change over time as technology evolves.

Genelis does not guarantee the uninterrupted availability, performance, or behavior of third-party AI systems.

Service interruptions or content variations may occur due to factors outside Genelis's control.

  • Refining workflows
  • Enhancing educational logic
  • Introducing new AI models
  • Improving content quality controls

As a result, outputs generated at different times may vary even when similar questions are submitted.
