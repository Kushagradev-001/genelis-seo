About Genelis

Genelis is an AI-powered educational learning platform primarily designed for students in Classes 9–12.

The Platform may provide:

  • AI-generated study notes
  • Subject-wise learning content
  • Chapter-wise learning content
  • AI-powered doubt solving
  • Mock tests
  • Practice assessments
  • Progress analytics
  • Learning recommendations
  • PDF downloads
  • Current affairs and educational resources
  • Additional educational features introduced from time to time

Genelis is currently operated by an individual proprietor and may subsequently be operated through a registered business entity, affiliate, successor, or permitted assignee.
