Introduction

This Refund & Cancellation Policy governs the purchase, renewal, cancellation, and refund of subscriptions offered by Genelis. By purchasing a subscription, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Policy. This Policy should be read together with the Genelis Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Subscription Plans

Genelis may offer various subscription plans, including but not limited to:

Monthly Plan

  • ₹149 per month

Annual Plan

  • ₹1199 per year

Genelis reserves the right to modify subscription plans, features, pricing, and benefits from time to time. Changes shall apply prospectively and shall not affect an active subscription already purchased by a user.

Cancellation of Subscriptions

Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their account settings (where available) or by contacting Genelis support. Cancellation will prevent future renewals but will not result in the immediate termination of access. Users will generally continue to enjoy subscription benefits until the end of the active subscription period.

Refund Policy

General Rule

All subscription purchases are final. Once a subscription has been activated and paid features have become available to the user, fees paid are generally non-refundable. This policy exists because users receive immediate access to digital educational services and content upon activation.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will generally not be provided in situations including, but not limited to:

Change of Mind

Where a user simply decides they no longer wish to use the Platform.

Partial Usage

Where a user has used some features but not others.

Lack of Usage

Where a user chooses not to access the Platform after purchasing a subscription.

Academic Expectations

Where the user is dissatisfied with examination results, academic performance, rankings, grades, admissions, or educational outcomes.

Device or Internet Issues

Where the inability to use the Platform is caused by:

  • User device limitations
  • Internet connectivity problems
  • Browser incompatibility
  • Third-party technical issues

Failure to Cancel Before Renewal

Where a renewal charge occurs after the user has failed to cancel an active subscription before the renewal date.

Exceptional Refund Circumstances

Genelis may, at its sole discretion, consider refund requests in exceptional circumstances, including:

Duplicate Payments

Where a user is charged multiple times for the same subscription due to a technical error.

Incorrect Billing

Where an incorrect amount has been charged due to a system issue.

Extended Platform Outage

Where a substantial service outage prevents meaningful access to paid features for an extended period and no reasonable alternative solution can be provided.

Regulatory Requirements

Where a refund is required by applicable law or competent authority.

Approval of any refund request remains subject to verification and review by Genelis.

Refund Review Process

Users seeking a refund under exceptional circumstances should contact:

Email: support@genelis.in

The request should include:

  • Full name
  • Registered email address
  • Transaction reference number
  • Date of purchase
  • Description of the issue

Genelis may request additional information reasonably necessary to investigate the request.

Processing of Approved Refunds

Where a refund is approved:

  • Refunds shall generally be processed through the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Processing timelines may vary depending on banks, payment gateways, UPI providers, card networks, and financial institutions.
  • Genelis is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment providers.
