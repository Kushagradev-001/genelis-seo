Purpose

Genelis is committed to providing a transparent, fair, and user-focused experience. This Grievance Redressal Policy establishes a framework through which users, parents, guardians, educators, subscribers, and other stakeholders may raise concerns, complaints, or requests relating to the Platform.

The objective of this Policy is to:

  • Promote transparency and accountability.
  • Provide an accessible complaint resolution process.
  • Address concerns in a timely and reasonable manner.
  • Improve user trust and service quality.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

This Policy should be read together with the:

  • Terms of Use
  • Privacy Policy
  • Refund & Cancellation Policy
  • AI Content Disclaimer & Responsible AI Policy
  • Copyright & Intellectual Property Policy

Who May Submit a Grievance

A grievance may be submitted by:

  • Registered users
  • Students
  • Parents
  • Legal guardians
  • Educators
  • Subscribers
  • Copyright owners
  • Authorized representatives
  • Any person affected by the use of the Platform

Genelis may request reasonable verification before processing certain requests.

Types of Grievances

Users may raise concerns relating to, including but not limited to:

Account-Related Issues

  • Login problems
  • Account access concerns
  • Unauthorized access
  • Account suspension
  • Account deletion requests

Subscription and Billing Issues

  • Subscription activation concerns
  • Billing discrepancies
  • Duplicate payments
  • Renewal issues
  • Refund requests
  • Payment failures

Educational Content Issues

  • Incorrect educational content
  • Outdated content
  • Broken resources
  • Technical issues affecting learning services

AI-Generated Content Concerns

  • Inaccurate AI-generated responses
  • Misleading educational outputs
  • Suspected AI malfunctions
  • Responsible AI concerns

Privacy and Data Protection Concerns

  • Access requests
  • Correction requests
  • Deletion requests
  • Consent-related concerns
  • Data processing complaints

Intellectual Property Concerns

  • Copyright infringement claims
  • Trademark concerns
  • Unauthorized use of intellectual property
  • Content ownership disputes

Platform Abuse and Security Issues

  • Fraudulent activity
  • Security vulnerabilities
  • Suspected hacking attempts
  • Abuse of platform features

Other Complaints

Any issue relating to the use of Genelis that is not specifically covered elsewhere in this Policy.

4. How to Submit a Grievance

Users may submit grievances by contacting:

Grievance Officer
Genelis
Email: support@genelis.in
Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India

To assist with investigation and resolution, users should provide:

  • Full name
  • Registered email address (if applicable)
  • Contact details
  • Description of the issue
  • Relevant dates
  • Screenshots or supporting documents (where available)
  • Transaction reference number (if applicable)

Incomplete information may delay resolution.

Additional Feedback and Support Channels

In addition to contacting the Grievance Officer by email, Genelis may provide an in-platform Feedback & Support section through which users may submit:

  • Feedback
  • Suggestions
  • Feature requests
  • Complaints
  • Technical issues
  • Educational content concerns
  • AI-generated content concerns
  • Subscription-related queries

Submissions made through the Feedback & Support section may be reviewed by the appropriate Genelis team and, where applicable, may be treated as grievances under this Policy.

The availability of such features does not limit a user's ability to directly contact the Grievance Officer through the contact details provided in this Policy.

5. Acknowledgement of Complaints

Genelis will make reasonable efforts to acknowledge receipt of grievances within:

Seven (7) business days from the date of receipt.

Acknowledgement does not imply acceptance of the grievance or guarantee any particular outcome.

6. Review and Investigation

Upon receiving a grievance, Genelis may:

  • Review relevant records.
  • Verify account information.
  • Examine platform logs.
  • Assess technical information.
  • Request additional documentation.
  • Consult internal teams or service providers.

Genelis reserves the right to determine the scope and method of investigation appropriate for the issue raised.

7. Resolution Timelines

Genelis will make reasonable efforts to resolve grievances within:

Thirty (30) calendar days from the date sufficient information is received.

Certain matters may require additional time, including:

  • Complex technical investigations
  • Security incidents
  • Payment disputes involving third parties
  • Intellectual property claims
  • Matters involving legal or regulatory review

Where additional time is required, Genelis may provide updates where reasonably practicable.

8. Possible Resolution Outcomes

Depending on the circumstances, resolutions may include:

  • Clarification of policies
  • Technical assistance
  • Error correction
  • Account restoration
  • Subscription adjustments
  • Refunds (where appropriate)
  • Content updates
  • Security measures
  • Account restrictions
  • Rejection of unsupported claims

The nature of the resolution will depend on the facts of each case.

9. Privacy of Grievances

Information submitted during the grievance process shall be handled in accordance with the Genelis Privacy Policy.

Genelis may use grievance information for:

  • Investigation
  • Resolution
  • Compliance
  • Security
  • Service improvement

Such information will not be disclosed except where necessary for resolution, legal compliance, or protection of rights.

10. False, Fraudulent, or Abusive Complaints

Genelis encourages genuine complaints and feedback.

Users shall not:

  • Submit knowingly false complaints.
  • Misrepresent facts.
  • Provide fraudulent information.
  • Abuse the grievance process.
  • Harass support personnel.

Genelis reserves the right to reject complaints that appear malicious, fraudulent, abusive, or submitted in bad faith.

11. Third-Party Issues

Certain services available through Genelis may involve third-party providers, including:

  • Payment processors
  • AI service providers
  • Hosting providers
  • Analytics services

Where a grievance primarily concerns a third-party service, Genelis may coordinate with the relevant provider but cannot guarantee outcomes controlled by third parties.

12. Regulatory and Legal Rights

Nothing in this Policy limits any rights available under applicable law, including rights relating to:

  • Consumer protection
  • Data protection
  • Contractual remedies
  • Intellectual property enforcement

Users remain free to pursue remedies available under applicable legal frameworks.

13. Policy Updates

Genelis may revise this Grievance Redressal Policy from time to time.

Updated versions shall become effective upon publication unless otherwise stated.

Continued use of the Platform following publication of updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

15. Our Commitment

Genelis values user trust and is committed to addressing concerns fairly, respectfully, and transparently.

We view grievances not only as complaints but also as opportunities to improve our educational services, technology, and user experience.
