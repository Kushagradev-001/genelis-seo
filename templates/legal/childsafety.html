1. Purpose

Genelis is committed to providing a safe, responsible, and educationally focused learning environment for students.

This Child & Student Safety Policy describes the principles, safeguards, and practices that guide how Genelis designs, operates, and manages its educational services for students.

This Policy should be read together with the:

  • Terms of Use
  • Privacy Policy
  • AI Content Disclaimer & Responsible AI Policy
  • Grievance Redressal Policy
  • Copyright & Intellectual Property Policy

2. Our Commitment to Student Safety

Genelis is designed with the primary objective of supporting student learning and academic growth.

We are committed to:

  • Prioritizing student welfare.
  • Respecting student privacy.
  • Promoting safe and responsible technology use.
  • Providing educationally relevant content.
  • Maintaining transparency regarding AI-generated content.
  • Supporting parents and guardians in supervising student learning.

Student well-being takes precedence over engagement metrics, advertising objectives, or commercial interests.

3. Who This Policy Applies To

This Policy applies to:

  • Students using Genelis.
  • Parents and guardians.
  • Educators interacting with the Platform.
  • Individuals accessing educational content intended for students.

Genelis is primarily designed for students approximately 13 to 19 years of age.

4. Education-First Design Principle

Genelis is designed as an educational platform.

Features are intended to:

  • Support learning.
  • Encourage revision.
  • Improve academic understanding.
  • Promote critical thinking.
  • Assist exam preparation.

Genelis does not intentionally design features to encourage excessive or unhealthy platform usage.

5. Parental Involvement

Genelis recognizes the important role of parents and guardians in a student's educational journey.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to:

  • Review their child's use of educational technology.
  • Monitor study habits.
  • Discuss AI-generated content with students.
  • Encourage responsible digital behavior.
  • Contact Genelis with concerns or questions.

Where required by applicable law, parental consent may be requested before certain services are provided.

6. Student Privacy Commitment

Genelis is committed to protecting student information.

We strive to:

  • Collect only information reasonably necessary to provide services.
  • Avoid unnecessary collection of personal information.
  • Maintain reasonable safeguards for student data.
  • Limit access to student information to authorized personnel and service providers.

Additional information regarding data practices is available in the Genelis Privacy Policy.

7. No Sale of Student Data

Genelis does not sell student personal information.

Student data will not be sold to:

  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing companies
  • Third-party commercial entities

Genelis believes student information should be used solely for educational and operational purposes.

8. No Behavioral Advertising to Students

Genelis does not currently display third-party advertisements.

Genelis does not intentionally engage in behavioral advertising directed at students.

If advertising models are introduced in the future, appropriate safeguards and disclosures will be implemented in accordance with applicable laws and policies.

9. Responsible Use of Artificial Intelligence

Genelis utilizes artificial intelligence to assist students with learning.

AI may be used to generate:

  • Notes
  • Summaries
  • Mock tests
  • Practice questions
  • Explanations
  • Doubt-solving responses

Genelis acknowledges that AI systems may occasionally generate inaccurate or incomplete content.

Accordingly:

  • AI outputs should be reviewed critically.
  • Students should verify important information.
  • AI should supplement, not replace, learning from textbooks and teachers.

Genelis seeks to promote responsible and informed use of AI technology.

10. Safe AI Interaction Guidelines

Students should avoid submitting:

  • Personal financial information
  • Government identification numbers
  • Passwords
  • Sensitive personal information
  • Medical information
  • Information relating to third parties without permission

Students are encouraged to use AI tools only for educational and appropriate purposes.

11. Content Standards

Genelis aims to provide content that is:

  • Educationally relevant.
  • Age-appropriate.
  • Respectful.
  • Academically focused.

Genelis does not intentionally provide content that:

  • Promotes violence.
  • Encourages discrimination.
  • Encourages harassment.
  • Promotes unlawful activities.
  • Exploits children or minors.
  • Contains sexually explicit material.

Users are encouraged to report any content they believe violates these standards.

12. Academic Integrity

Genelis encourages ethical learning practices.

Students should use Genelis to:

  • Learn concepts.
  • Practice questions.
  • Improve understanding.
  • Prepare for examinations.

Students should not use Genelis to:

  • Cheat during examinations.
  • Violate school policies.
  • Submit AI-generated work as entirely their own where disclosure is required.
  • Engage in academic misconduct.

Genelis promotes learning, not academic dishonesty.

13. Student Well-Being

Genelis encourages healthy and balanced use of educational technology.

Students are encouraged to:

  • Take regular study breaks.
  • Balance online and offline learning.
  • Seek assistance from teachers and parents when needed.
  • Maintain healthy study habits.

Genelis supports educational success through responsible learning practices.

14. Reporting Safety Concerns

Students, parents, guardians, and educators may report concerns relating to:

  • Student safety
  • Privacy
  • Inappropriate content
  • AI-generated content issues
  • Harassment
  • Security concerns
  • Platform misuse

Reports may be submitted through:

  • The Feedback & Support section of the Platform
  • Email: support@genelis.in

Genelis will make reasonable efforts to review and address concerns appropriately.

15. Platform Misuse

Genelis may restrict or terminate access where users:

  • Abuse platform services.
  • Attempt to exploit AI systems.
  • Engage in harassment.
  • Submit malicious content.
  • Interfere with platform security.
  • Violate applicable policies.

Such actions may be taken to protect students, users, and the Platform.

16. Continuous Improvement

Genelis recognizes that technology, educational practices, and safety expectations evolve over time.

Accordingly, Genelis may periodically review and improve:

  • Student safety practices.
  • AI safeguards.
  • Privacy protections.
  • Educational content standards.
  • Platform security measures.

17. Policy Updates

Genelis may update this Child & Student Safety Policy from time to time.

Updated versions shall become effective upon publication unless otherwise stated.

Continued use of the Platform following publication of updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

19. Our Student Safety Promise

Genelis believes that educational technology should empower students, support families, and promote learning in a safe and responsible manner.

We are committed to building a platform that prioritizes education, transparency, privacy, safety, and responsible innovation for every learner who uses Genelis.
