2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when visiting a website or using certain online services.

Cookies help websites:

  • Recognize users.
  • Remember preferences.
  • Maintain login sessions.
  • Improve functionality.
  • Understand platform performance.
  • Enhance user experience.

Cookies generally do not provide access to information stored elsewhere on a user's device.
